Mac and Cheese Recipe

This is a classic American dish that is loved by the whole family. In my household we love to make it for all the family get togethers that we have over the year. Nothing bring together a family summer BBQ like this dish.

With a couple simple ingredients and a little time your family will be skipping the packaged stuff at the store and wanting to make this instead.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of your choice of pasta
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 cups of shredded mild cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded munster cheese
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs

Steps:

  1. Preheat the over to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large pot boil 8 cups of water.
  3. When the water comes to a boil add the pasta and cook the pasta for 10 minutes.
  4. Drian the pasta and put it in a large baking dish and set aside.
  5. In a medium pot over high heat add the butter.
  6. When the butter has melted add the flour and stir with a wisk until all the butter is gone.
  7. Slowly add the milk and continue to stir until all the milk is add and you done have any lumps.
  8. Bring you mixtue to a boil and add your cheese and continue to mix until your cheese have melted and everything is well blended together.
  9. Add your cheese mixtue to your pasta in the baking dish and mix well.
  10. Cover the top with your bread crumps.
  11. Place in the over and bake for 35 mintues.
  12. Once done let sit for 10 minutes and enjoy
