Pulled Pork

When I travel to the Carolinas I make to a habit to stop and try some pulled pork. I wanted to try and make it so I could have it any time that I wanted to. This is a recipe that I came up with with a little trial and error. Having some friends that are from the area helps with getting everything right. This recipe is for a croke pot so I can make this anytime of the year.

Ingredients:

  • 4 to 5 pound boneless prok butt shoulder
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons of oinion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 or 3 bay leafs
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
